What is a Lead?
An individual or family who has expressed interest in Intercare's ABA therapy services through the website, a social media campaign, a referring physician, or an outreach coordinator.
What is a Conversion?
A lead accepted into Salesforce who has begun the assessment, authorization, and/or scheduling process to get their ABA therapy launch scheduled.
